How Does It Work?

Step 1. Open Verde

The Verde is a flexible solution that can be accessed through MS Teams, PowerApps and USSD. This flexibility makes it compatibale with various industries.

Step 2. Complete The Health Check

Verde's self diagnosis tool presents a series of questions to assess employee health and then automatically actions company policy.

Step 3. Proof of Vaccine or Objection

Employees can upload proof of vaccination or lodge an objection through the legally required steps to ensure legal compliance.

Step 4. Risk Management

Verde provides health risk management notifications to ensure that administrators are aware of the risk to thier workforce and overall productivity.
